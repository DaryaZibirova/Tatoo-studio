HOW WE WORK

1. Consultation

We will help you choose a sketch, then we will apply it to your body to determine the place of tattooing. Also we will discuss prices and the number of sessions. We can develop a sketch.

2. Creating a sketch

We will create a sketch taking into account all your wishes.

3. Session

We will draw a sketch, prepare a sterile place and do the work.

4. Healing

We will give you recommendations on healing your skin afterwards.

5. Correction

Most of the times, a new tattoo needs correction after the first session

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The cost of the work is estimated by its size and complexity. The complexity of tattooing depends on the place on the body and the number of small elements. The minimum price of a tattoo is 2000 rubles.

The first correction after healing is almost always necessary, because the pigment can fall out regardless of the professionalism of the specialist and the quality of the equipment. Especially when we talk about color tattoos or complex drawings.

The cost of the first correction within 2 months starts from 1000 to 5000 rubles (depending on the amount of work).
